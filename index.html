👋 Chairman's Opening Remarks

Well, where has the year gone? 🙄 Just like that, we're into August and heading into the latter part of the season!

There's still plenty of racing ahead, so get involved and join your fellow clubmates. The recent buzz at races has been electric – it’s fantastic to be out there competing alongside each other.

As mentioned below, the Loch Ore Triathlon Festival is now just 4 weeks away. It would be brilliant to see our biggest-ever TriBridges turnout! Last time was an absolute blast – whether you’re racing or cheering, come along and be part of it.

A huge thanks to our committee for their sterling work behind the scenes. Running a club takes serious effort to keep everything ticking over, and their dedication doesn’t go unnoticed.

Finally, a warm welcome to Doug and Jake who’ve recently joined, and best of luck to Charlie racing at Ironman Sweden in a couple of weeks!

🏊 Open Water Clinic – 5th August

Join our FREE Open Water Swim Clinic at Lochore Meadows – perfect for all abilities!

  • 📅 Date & Time: Tuesday 5th August (evening session)
  • 📍 Location: Lochore Meadows
  • 👨‍🏫 Focus: Wetsuit tips, entry/exit skills, sighting, drafting & confidence building
  • ✅ All abilities welcome – from beginners to experienced triathletes
  • 💰 Cost: FREE for all members
