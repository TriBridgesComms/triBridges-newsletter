Norseman Xtreme Triathlon

Claire Weller secured a superb 4th place in an elite field at one of the worlds most iconic races. Reflecting afterwards, she said: Norseman 100% pushed me to my limits. It was a challenging but amazing experience. Norway is a beautiful country and racing there was a privilege.

Never one to shy away from gruelling challenges, Claire now turns her focus to Ironman Wales next month known as the dragon and considered one of the toughest Ironman courses in the world.

Good luck Claire from everyone at TriBridges!
