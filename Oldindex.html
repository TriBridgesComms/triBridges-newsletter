🗞️ Race Highlights

Ironman Sweden — Kalmar 🇸🇪💪

Charlie Crawford took on his first full-distance Ironman in Kalmar and delivered an outstanding performance, especially impressive after overcoming injury setbacks in the run-up. A fantastic debut cheered on by a huge Swedish crowd.

📖 Read Charlie’s full race report

Ironman Switzerland — Thun 🇨🇭

In 2025, Keith delivered a fantastic performance at Ironman Switzerland, finishing in 12:03:36. The beautiful course packs in around 2,000 m of elevation on the bike, making it a true test of endurance across the Swiss landscape.

📖 Read Keith’s race report

Norseman Xtreme Triathlon 🇳🇴🐉

Claire Weller secured a superb 4th place in an elite field at one of the world’s most iconic races. Reflecting afterwards, she said: “Norseman 100% pushed me to my limits. It was a challenging but amazing experience. Norway is a beautiful country and racing there was a privilege.”

Never one to shy away from gruelling challenges, Claire now turns her focus to Ironman Wales next month — known as “the dragon” and considered one of the toughest Ironman courses in the world.

💛 Good luck Claire from everyone at TriBridges!

Scottish Middle Distance Champs — Aberfeldy 🏆🏴

Heather Elder delivered a commanding performance at Aberfeldy, taking the win and earning the title of Scottish Middle Distance Champion. A brilliant day for both Heather and the club.
